About Us

Vision

"To develop the core values amongst the students while inculcating the feeling of nationalism, sense of pride, and commitment towards positive initiatives for the society"

Mission

To provide a platform for SVNITians, where they can get proper guidance and support irrespective of the domain they are working in.

Objectives

- Student Development: Providing a platform for the overall development of students through interaction with experts & established personalities.
- Student Alumni Network
- Problem Solving Approach

What we Believe
"सर्वभूत हि ते रता:"

The words sarva-bhūta-hite ratāḥ mean those who always wish for all living beings the same happiness and righteousness as they would wish for themselves.
